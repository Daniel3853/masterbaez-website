What Makes Master Baez the Best Martial Arts School in Cooper City, FL?

We are a child development center disguised as a martial arts school. Our hybrid program intersects fitness with identity development, giving athletes a training foundation that simultaneously builds practical life skills.

  • Expert Coaches, Not Volunteers. Every instructor is a trained mentor with deep understanding of athletic development and behavioral science.
  • A Curriculum Built Around Character. Every class blends martial arts technique with life lessons: focus, respect, emotional regulation, resilience.
  • A Community That Feels Like Family. "We Over Me" isn't just a slogan. It's how we train.
  • Real, Measurable Progress. No participation trophies. Your kid earns every level, every belt, every breakthrough.
  • A World-Class Facility. Clean, modern, energy-packed. Kids rise to the level of their environment.
