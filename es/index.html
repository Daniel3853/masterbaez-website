FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is martial arts good for shy kids? ▼
Absolutely! Martial arts is one of the best activities for shy children. Our structured, supportive environment helps them come out of their shell at their own pace. Many of our most confident students started as shy kids who barely wanted to make eye contact.
Can martial arts help kids with ADHD or focus issues? ▼
Yes! Martial arts is highly effective for kids with ADHD. The structured environment, clear expectations, and physical activity help channel energy productively while building focus and self-regulation skills.
What age should my child start martial arts? ▼
We offer programs starting at age 4 with our Tiny Tigers program. Every age group has a curriculum designed specifically for their developmental stage.
How is Master Baez different from other martial arts schools in Cooper City? ▼
Master Baez combines elite athletic training with character development in a way no other school does. We use modern, gamified approaches, neuroscience-backed methods, and we truly partner with parents to develop confident leaders.
What if my child doesn't like it? ▼
We offer a 100% money-back guarantee. If your child doesn't love their first week, we'll refund every penny. No questions asked. We're that confident in what we do.
How long until I see results? ▼
Many parents notice changes in confidence and attitude within the first few weeks. The real transformation builds over months as your child progresses through our system.
My child has never done martial arts before. Will they feel behind? ▼
Not at all! Every champion starts as a beginner. Our coaches are experts at welcoming new students and meeting them where they are. Everyone progresses at their own pace.
Is martial arts violent? I don't want my child becoming aggressive. ▼
Martial arts teaches self-control, discipline, and respect. Our students learn that violence is never the answer. In fact, martial arts builds confidence in a way that reduces the likelihood of bullying or aggressive behavior.
